somnitelnyye prichiny zhit

Zdes my rasskazyvaem v litsakh, pochemu zhizn - ponyatiye kompleksnoye, no ot togo ne menee zakhvatyvayushee, dazhe esli kazhetsya, chto v bytovyh usloviyah teryat uzhe nechego. Sredi soten intervyu s raznymi, na pervy vzglyad obychnymi lyudmi est mnozhestvo raznyh prichin ne sdavatsya, i, nesmotrya na vse slozhnosti, nas vseh - posetitelnits, intervyuerov i intervyuiruemykh, est din obyedinyayushiy faktor - pokazat, chto geroi ne nosyat plashey, ne spasayut goroda vystrelami iz glaz, a spravlyayutsya so mnojestvom prepyatstviy i prodolzhayut tvorit, lyubit i derzhatsya vmeste. My nadeemsya, chto eti istorii, passkazannyye samimi lyudmi, pomogut vam uvidet svet v kontse tunnelya i ponyat, chto vy ne odni.
